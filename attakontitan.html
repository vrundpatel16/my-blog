Attack on Titan: Humanity’s Last Stand

Explore the world beyond the Walls—where Titans roam and freedom is a distant dream.

Eren Yeager: The Catalyst of Freedom and Destruction

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager began as a passionate boy driven by a desire to protect his friends and escape the confines of the Walls. But as truths unraveled and burdens mounted, he transformed into a complex figure—both hero and antagonist. As the inheritor of the Attack and Founding Titans, Eren challenged fate itself, seeking freedom at any cost. His journey is one of rage, sacrifice, and haunting conviction, leaving behind a legacy that reshaped the world.

Erwin Smith: Vision Beyond the Walls

Erwin Smith was a leader who bore the weight of impossible choices. As Commander of the Survey Corps, he inspired unwavering loyalty and led countless expeditions into Titan territory. Driven by a thirst for truth and a dream of freedom, Erwin sacrificed everything—including his own life—to uncover the secrets of their world. His legacy lives on in the hearts of those who followed him, and in the truths he died to reveal.

Mikasa Ackerman: Silent Blade, Unbreakable Bond

Mikasa Ackerman is a warrior forged by tragedy and bound by love. Adopted into the Yeager family after a brutal loss, she became Eren’s fiercest protector—driven not by duty, but by devotion. As a descendant of the Ackerman bloodline, she possesses superhuman strength and combat instincts, making her one of humanity’s deadliest fighters. Beneath her stoic exterior lies a heart that quietly carries the weight of every battle, every goodbye.

Armin Arlert: The Mind That Moved Mountains

Armin Arlert may have lacked physical strength, but his intellect and empathy made him indispensable. From the boy who dreamed of the ocean to the inheritor of the Colossal Titan, Armin’s journey is marked by painful choices and quiet courage. His strategies turned the tide of war, and his voice often reminded others of their humanity. In a world consumed by fear and vengeance, Armin stood as a beacon of reason—and the hope for a better future.

Captain Levi: Humanity’s Strongest Soldier

Levi Ackerman is the embodiment of discipline and lethality. Raised in the underground and hardened by loss, he rose to become humanity’s most formidable warrior. With unmatched agility and ruthless efficiency, Levi has slain more Titans than any other soldier. Yet beneath his cold demeanor lies a deep sense of loyalty and justice. Whether facing Titans or traitors, Levi fights not for glory—but for the lives that still remain.
